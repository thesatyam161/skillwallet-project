About The Project

Redefining Retail Strategy

The Strategic Product Positioning Analysis System is a comprehensive data science project designed to uncover the hidden patterns in retail behavior.

Problem Statement

Retail businesses struggle to optimize product placement effectively relying on intuition rather than data. Poor product visibility and sub-optimal store layouts lead to lower conversion rates and missed revenue opportunities.

Project Objectives

Analyze Relationships

Correlate product placement with sales performance, conversion rates, and visibility scores.

Understand Consumers

Map demographic data (age, gender) against purchasing behavior and store traffic.

Process Big Data

Handle complex datasets, implement data cleaning, and generate custom calculated fields.

Interactive Platform

Deliver insights through a modern Flask web application backed by a consistent Plotly and SQLite analytics stack.

Technology Stack

Python Flask Plotly HTML5/CSS3 Bootstrap 5 SQLite/MySQL
